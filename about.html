About

I am a Machine Learning Research Engineer currently working on different research projects mostly related to deep learning applied to remote sensing and astronomy. I have been serving as the CTO and Co-Director of the Center for Data and Artificial Intelligence At University of Concepción since 2021.

In my penultimate year of Engineering, I won a fully-funded scholarship to hold my MSc. in Computer Science at UdeC. Pursuing this program, I was advised by Guillermo Cabrera-Vives and co-advised by Pavlos Protopapas. With their support, I was able to do my undergraduate honor research thesis (currently published at PASP), and my master thesis (honored with the Best Student Paper Award at Astroinformatics2019 conference).

What moves me

For me, working is how we pay people back, what they do for us. Therefore, the more we do what we love, the more we give love back to the world and to the people who live in it.

One day, trying to find the best way for me to give back to the world, I concluded what can be summarized in three words: apply, generate and spread.

Applying is, in my opinion, the best way to learn. When some knowledge is acquired, it opens up the possibility to materialize new ideas, thereby generating new knowledge. However, none of these words have any sense if I am not capable of spreading what I have learned and making it available to other people.

In my spare time, I enjoy different hobbies and passions such as dancing, surfing, cycling, and playing drums ❤️.
