@UDS

Unidad de Data Science (UDS), UdeC.

While I was doing my grad research thesis, I was asked by my advisor to participate in a project for ARAUCO Company, which consisted of counting and segmenting trees using georeferenced hyperspectral images taken by drones. As I really like challenging problems, I accepted immediately.

The project progressed quickly, and satisfactory results were achieved in a short period of time. After that, ARAUCO signed a one-year service contract and the UDS was founded by my advisor as an spin-off of the school of Engineering.

Unfortunately in that period I was living in Boston, but when I came back the UDS was already working with amazing people. We resume the previous problem but now even more challenging: the design and implementation of a Cloud-based plataform for labeling, training and prediction to count, detect and segment georeferenced drone and satellite RGB images.

The project was succesfully finished and now I am doing other interesting projects, but always supporting UDS, specially in post-production, client contact, and guiding new projects.

What is UDS?

Is a spin-off of the school of Engineering of UdeC, dedicated to the processing, modelling and analysis of data, generating specialized and innovative solutions for the companies issues. The main goal is to bridge the gap between the academy and industry and make our products help in decision making inside the companies.

Moreover, we count with the support of more than 15 Professors from different areas such as sensorization, data analysis, software engineering, artficial intelligence, simulation, and operation research.

What we offer?

Projects
We develop and implement state-of-the-art solutions that involves modelling and data analysis, thereby helping the company in decision making.
Consultancy
We solve complex problem associated with data handling and treatment. We think in better ways to extract, storage and distribute useful data inside the company.
Capacitation
We train technical skills and abilities to use best practices in the data manipulation. We offer two academic programs oriented to industry professionals: data science diploma and advanced data science diploma.
